Turbulence Research Laboratory

The Turbulence Research Lab focuses on enhancing the understanding of the physics underlying turbulence phenomenon, and developing numerical methods for efficient and high-fidelity turbulence simulation. Large-scale multi-processor paralleled programs are developed for turbulence DNS and LES, implementing high-accuracy finite difference schemes and/or spectral methods. Various types of turbulent flow, including isotropic turbulence, channel flow, boundary layer, rough wall turbulence, particle laden turbulence, high- Mach compressible flow and complex fluid-solid coupling problems, are under active research.
